Suit Indonesia

Suit Indonesia is another variation of the game "Suit" (like Rock, Paper, Scissors) with a different elements. In this game, there are three main choices : Elephant, Human, and Ant. Each option has different strengths

“Jari Kelilngking”
as Ant

“Jari Telunjuk”
as Human

“Ibu Jari”
as Elephant

RULES

VS

Elephant beats People: Elephants are stronger and can trample people.

VS

People beat Ants: People can step on ants because they are bigger.

VS

Ant beats Elephant: The ant can get into the elephant's ear and disturb it, so the ant wins.

RULES

  1. The game consists of 3 rounds.
  2. The player who wins 2 consecutive rounds is immediately declared the winner.
  3. If the first two rounds result in a tie, the game proceeds to a third round
